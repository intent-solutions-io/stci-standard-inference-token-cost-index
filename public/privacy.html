Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Inference Price Index ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our website and API services at inferencepriceindex.com (the "Service").

2. Information We Collect

API Usage Data

When you access our API, we may collect:

  • IP address and request timestamps
  • API endpoints accessed and request frequency
  • User-Agent headers and referrer information

Website Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics (provided by Google) to understand how visitors use our site. This service collects:

  • Device information and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referral source
  • General geographic location (country/region level)
  • Session and engagement data

Firebase Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to collect this data. You can opt out by enabling "Do Not Track" in your browser or using browser extensions that block analytics scripts.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identifying information (names, emails) - unless you contact us
  • Payment information - our API is free
  • Cookies for tracking or advertising

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Monitor API usage patterns and prevent abuse
  • Improve service performance and reliability
  • Generate aggregate, anonymized usage statistics
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: Cloud hosting (Google Cloud Platform) and infrastructure services
  • Legal compliance: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Aggregate data: Anonymized, aggregate statistics may be published

5. Data About LLM Pricing

The pricing data we publish is collected from publicly available sources including official provider pricing pages and aggregator APIs. This data is factual information about commercial pricing and does not constitute personal data.

All pricing observations include full provenance metadata (source URL, collection timestamp) to ensure transparency and verifiability.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure cloud infrastructure (Google Cloud Platform)
  • Regular security reviews
  • Access controls and monitoring

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

API access logs are retained for 90 days for security and abuse prevention. Aggregate analytics data may be retained indefinitely. Pricing observation data is retained as a permanent historical record.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of any personal data (e.g., if you contacted us)
  • Opt out of analytics tracking by using browser privacy settings

9. Third-Party Services

Our Service uses:

  • Google Cloud Platform / Firebase: Hosting and infrastructure
  • Firebase Analytics: Website usage analytics (Google Privacy Policy)
  • GitHub: Source code and issue tracking

These services have their own privacy policies which govern their data practices.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
